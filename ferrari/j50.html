Full Description

Ferrari J50 is a one-of-10 limited edition supercar featuring a stunning targa-top design and advanced aerodynamics derived from Ferrari’s racing heritage. Its aggressive exterior features sharp lines, wide stance, LED headlights, and signature Ferrari styling cues that make it instantly recognizable.

The interior is luxurious and driver-focused, with premium leather, Alcantara trim, carbon-fiber elements, and a digital cockpit designed for ultimate performance and comfort.

The 3.9L V8 twin-turbo engine produces 690 hp and 760 Nm of torque, delivering blistering acceleration and an exhilarating exhaust note. Performance features include advanced traction control, adaptive suspension, carbon-ceramic brakes, and precise handling systems.

Safety systems include ABS, stability control, multiple airbags, and high-performance braking technology, ensuring a secure yet thrilling driving experience.

Overall, Ferrari J50 is a rare masterpiece for collectors and enthusiasts seeking unmatched performance, exclusivity, and Italian craftsmanship. It represents Ferrari’s legacy of innovation, style, and speed.
