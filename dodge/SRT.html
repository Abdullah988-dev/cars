Full Description

Dodge SRT is a high-performance version of Dodge’s legendary muscle cars, engineered by the Street & Racing Technology division. It features an aggressive wide-body stance, functional hood scoops, large air intakes, and bold SRT badging that instantly sets it apart.

The interior of Dodge SRT focuses on both comfort and performance. It comes with sport leather seats, flat-bottom steering wheel, aluminum pedals, and a driver-centric dashboard. The advanced infotainment system includes a large touchscreen, performance pages, navigation, Apple CarPlay, and Android Auto.

Dodge SRT is equipped with high-performance Brembo brakes, adaptive suspension, launch control, traction control, rear-view camera, and multiple airbags. These features ensure maximum control, safety, and confidence even at high speeds.

Overall, Dodge SRT represents the ultimate combination of raw power, racing technology, and aggressive design. It is perfect for drivers who want an adrenaline-filled driving experience with unmistakable American muscle character.
